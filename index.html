Our first product Pamoja · For Foundations & NGOs

Managing a scholarship
programme? Meet Pamoja.

Pamoja is the platform we built for education foundations that manage students, sponsors, and schools. It started as a custom build for Gakundi Memorial Foundation in Kenya and we now offer it to any foundation ready to leave spreadsheets behind.

From student dossiers and academic tracking to AI-powered analysis and auto-generated sponsor presentations. Pamoja does the administration so your team can focus on the people you serve.
