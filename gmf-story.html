The before:
fragile by design

For most of GMF's history, managing 123 students across 47 schools meant one thing: an overwhelming amount of manual work. Every hour spent on administration was an hour not spent on a student.

🗂️

Files scattered across drives

Student dossiers as PDFs across local hard drives and shared folders no naming convention, no search, no version control.

📊

Academic results in Excel

Manually updated spreadsheets, prone to error, impossible to query across cohorts or years.

📧

Donor reports rebuilt every time

Copy-pasting data, hunting for photos, rebuilding context from scratch for every sponsor update.

🔎

No early warning system

A student missing school due to unpaid fees, like Simon Ngunjiri, could go unnoticed until it was too late to help.

The after:
a living platform

Galago built a purpose-designed web application around GMF's actual rhythms: from initial student selection through four years of secondary school and beyond. Every feature replaced a specific manual process.

📊

Live Impact Dashboard

All 123 students, grade distribution, gender breakdown, school coverage: recalculated in real time. Always current.

Replaces: weekly check-in emails
🎓

Student Profiles & Digital Dossiers

Every student's full history in one click: family background, primary results, every report card, photos, mentor notes.

Replaces: scattered PDFs & drives
🤖

AI Performance Analysis

The platform reads academic data and flags students who may be struggling before it becomes a crisis.

Replaces: reactive spreadsheet review
🔗

Living Partnership Deck

A full-screen presentation that pulls from live data. Share a link: real students, real results, always up to date.

Replaces: rebuilt PowerPoints
